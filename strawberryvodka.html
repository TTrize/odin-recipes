Strawberry Vodka

/

Ingredients

  • 1/2 glass of vodka
  • 1 glass of water
  • 5 frozen Strawberry
  • condensed milk(your preference).

Prepare

Drik

In a blender, beat the vodka and water. Adding condensed milk to your taste, at the end you can add sugar to break up the acidity of the vodka.

In your glass adding ice and strawberry for final drink

Finaly you have a good drink.
