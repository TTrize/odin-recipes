RamenNoodles Gourmet

/

Ingredients

  • 450 ml of water
  • 1 package of noodles
  • 1 slice of chopped ham
  • 1 slice of chopped turkey breast
  • 3 slices of chopped salami
  • 1/2 chopped tomato
  • 4 chopped lettuce leaves
  • 1/2 tablespoon of gorgonzola cheese
  • 2 spoons of cottage cheese
  • 1 slice of cheese
  • 1 packet of noodles seasoning

Prepare

Noodles

Put the water in the pen and turn on the heat;

When the water starts to boil, add the pasta and let it cook for 3 minutes;

Add the ham, turkey breast, salami, tomato and lettuce. Mix;

Add the gorgonzola cheese, cottage cheese and cheese. Mix;

Insert the seasoning sachet, mix and turn off the heat;

Finally eat.
