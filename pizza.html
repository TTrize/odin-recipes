Chiken Pizza

Ingredients


Pasta

  • 1 cup of milk
  • 2 cup of flour
  • 1 dessert spoon of yeast
  • 2 tablespoons of oil
  • 1 pinch of salt

Sauce

  • 1 sachet of pizza flavored sauce
  • 1 sachet of bolognese sauce

Filling

  • 1 chicken breast
  • 10 slices of cheese

Chicken seasoning

  • 1/2 colher de sopa de cominho com pimenta
  • 1/2 tablespoon of paprika
  • 1 chicken knorr cube

To Cook the Chicken

  • 1 tomato
  • 1 piece of pepper
  • 1/2 onion

Prepare

Pasta

Mix the milk, flour and yeast in a bowl.

Then mix the oil and salt with the previous ingredients until you get a thick dough.

Open in a round pizza shape.

Sauce

Mix the pizza sauce with the bolognese sauce in a pan, cover and place in the fridge.

Pour the sauce over the pasta.

Filling

Place the cheese on top of the sauce. Cook the chicken with the tomato, papper and onion.

When it's cooked shred it and put it on the pizza

Sprinkle oregano.

Bake in the oven for 20 minutes at 280Â°C.
